Blossom

For this piece, we began exploring ways to use audio to interact with the artwork.
By applying noise operators to a sphere, we created a form that, when meshed, resembles a blossoming flower.
The audio-reactive element of this creation is highly versatile, offering greater flexibility than motion capture.
This innovation has the potential to enhance any art piece we produce.

Our goal is to introduce this technology in music venues and develop additional applications to expand its use and impact.
