Emanate




Our second audioreactive piece focuses on the synergy of the components of music in the digital age.

When sound is stored in the mp3 format, each sample is digitized, and the resulting sound is a combination of each individual component all emanating from the same source.

To that effect, melodic synths are considered visually with a mass of rounded shapes and the harsh, percussive hi-hats interrupt as sharp lines shooting out the screen all emanating from one central source.
